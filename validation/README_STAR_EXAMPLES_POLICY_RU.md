# Starred examples policy

`Example 3*` and `Example 4*` in `Examples_for_IBP_parametric.nb` are value-level examples.

They provide:
- an initial integral;
- an analytic answer / epsilon expansion.

They do not provide:
- `JIntegrandList`;
- `JCoefficientListt`;
- an IBP certificate;
- a decomposition into locally finite integrands.

Therefore, do not use them as e2e reducer expected outputs. Use them only as parser/sparse-polynomial fixtures and future value-level checks.
